We Have Professionals

Our expert architects and designers bring creativity and precision to every project, ensuring exceptional results from concept to completion.

Bernard Arnault

Bernard Arnault is the CEO of Rinterio, a leading force in innovative and sustainable architectural design. A graduate of the Manchester School of Architecture, Bernard combines creative vision with practical expertise, steering Rinterio to success across commercial, residential, and public projects. Under his leadership, the firm has earned a reputation for cutting-edge designs that prioritize both aesthetics and environmental responsibility. Passionate about mentoring the next generation, Bernard remains an influential figure in the architectural community.

❮ ❯

Ryan Smith

Ryan Smith, Managing Director of Rinterio, is a graduate of the University of California, Berkeley (UCB). With a strong foundation in architecture and design, Ryan leads Rinterio’s strategic and operational efforts, driving innovation and sustainability in every project. His leadership has cemented Rinterio's reputation for excellence, while his commitment to mentoring young architects underscores his dedication to the future of the industry.

❮ ❯

Jay Slater

Jay Slater, the Chief Architect at Rinterio, is a distinguished graduate of the Manchester School of Architecture. Known for his innovative design approach, Jay leads the creative direction of Rinterio’s architectural projects, blending aesthetics with functionality to create impactful spaces. His expertise and vision have been instrumental in establishing Rinterio as a leader in sustainable and cutting-edge architecture.
